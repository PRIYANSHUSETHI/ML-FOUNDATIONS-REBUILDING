# 🧠 What Is a Tensor? — The Building Block Behind Every Deep Learning Model

This repository accompanies my Medium article on understanding **tensors**, one of the most fundamental concepts in deep learning.

Although the term *tensor* often sounds intimidating at first, it is simply a structured way of storing numerical data. Every modern deep learning framework—whether TensorFlow or PyTorch—represents data internally as tensors.

In this article, I break down tensors from first principles, explain how they differ from scalars, vectors, and matrices, and show how higher-dimensional tensors naturally represent real-world data such as images, videos, and time-series.

## 🎯 Why This Matters

Tensors are the universal data structure used throughout deep learning. Whether you're building an image classifier, forecasting demand, processing text, or analyzing videos, your data eventually becomes a tensor.

Understanding tensors early makes learning neural networks, TensorFlow, and PyTorch significantly easier.
